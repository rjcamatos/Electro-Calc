Colpitts Oscillator Calculator

Brief for Colpitts Oscillator Circuit:

The Colpitts oscillator is represented as a tank circuit composed of an inductor and two capacitors, with the capacitors connected in series.

In this case, an NPN transistor is used to maintain the oscillation of the tank circuit.

ATTENTION: Run simulattion with: Start external DC Supply Voltages at 0V.
