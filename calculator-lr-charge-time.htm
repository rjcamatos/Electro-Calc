LR Charge Time Calculator

Brief for LR Charge Time Calculator:

Compute the L (inductor), R (resistor) or t (time) for a inductor and resistor in series, powered by a DC voltage.

Fill two of the three fields to compute the desired value, the inductance, the resistance or the time.


Brief for LR Charge Time Circuit:

The LR Charge Time Circuit consists of an inductor and resistor in series powered by a DC volatge, when time is 0 seconds (trun on the switch), it starts charging until it reach ~99% of the supply voltage (τ5), at that time maximum current (acounting with resistance) is passing through the circuit.
